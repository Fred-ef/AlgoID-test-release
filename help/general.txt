
usage:  algoid <command>

    Supported commands:

    > create:   Generates a new AlgoID identity and stores it in a secure wallet
    > resolve:  Visualizes all attributes related to the specified AlgoID identity
    > manage:   Manage your AlgoID identity by adding or removing attributes
    > delete:   Deactivates an existing AlgoID identity and deletes the associated material
    > vc:       Create, sign and verify Verifiable Credentials
    > sign:     Signs binary data with the specified AlgoID identity
    > verify:   Verifies binary data against the specified AlgoID identity
    > help:     Prints a guide for the command given
